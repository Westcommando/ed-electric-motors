About Us

Who We Are

ED Electric Motors is a proudly South African business built on decades of hands-on experience in electric motor repairs, rewinding, and servicing. Our story is rooted in quality, commitment, and family values.

Meet Our Founder

Edward Mashuro has been in the electric motor industry for over 30 years, working with leading companies like Delba. His deep technical knowledge and passion for motors have helped countless clients restore, rebuild, and maintain their machines with precision and care.

With Edward’s leadership, ED Electric Motors has become a trusted name for reliable service, skilled craftsmanship, and personalized support.

What Sets Us Apart

  • 🛠 Experience: Over three decades of professional motor work.
  • 💬 Direct Support: We communicate with clients personally and clearly.
  • ⚙️ Quality Workmanship: We never rush repairs — we do them right.
  • 📍 Local Commitment: Based in Springs, we serve the surrounding area with pride.
  • 👨‍👦 Family Legacy: Built by hand, passed on through generations.

Our Promise

We promise to treat every job — big or small — with the care, discipline, and attention it deserves. Whether you’re a business, factory, or private client, you can expect expert service every time.
